My Favorite Music Videos

Track One“Crazy” – Aerosmith

“Crazy” is one of Aerosmith’s most iconic videos, mixing storytelling, attitude, and energy. The video’s rebellious vibe and memorable characters make it a classic visually and musically.

Track Two“I Don't Wanna Miss a Thing” – Aerosmith

This song became one of Aerosmith’s biggest hits, and the music video adds strong cinematic emotion. Its visuals match the power of the song, making it a timeless classic.

Track Three“Simple Man” – Lynyrd Skynyrd

“Simple Man” is emotional and meaningful, with a message about life and values. The video’s reflective tone adds depth to the song’s powerful lyrics and gives it a timeless feel.
